Spiegel im Spiegel

I had the idea for this audio project years ago when I was listening to one of my favorite pieces of music on Youtube and noticed that the comment section was really special. This led to me reaching out to a bunch of the commentors and finally getting in contact with one person who wanted to share their story about what the music meant to them.
