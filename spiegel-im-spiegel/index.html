Spiegel im Spiegel

I had the idea for this audio project years ago when I was listening to one of my favorite pieces of music on Youtube and noticed something really interesting in the comments section. This led to me reaching out to a bunch of the commentors and finally getting in contact with one person who wanted to share their story about what the music meant to them.

This is my first attempt at doing a podcast type thing like this. If you actually make podcasts and want to give me some tips, I'd love to hear from you. Also if you have comments on what this music means to you, I'd also love to hear from you.
